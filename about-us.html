About Us

FROB – Friends of Books is a social platform for readers. Here you can find book clubs across various genres and can also start your own book club and thus not only be a part of a community but also create one. FROB is for the deep bonding that is formed based on reading lists with common interests and subjects.

We are a bunch of curious people who go by the book in their lives and are on a mission to bring people together not basis what they see and hear only but also what they read themselves and thus build a clear understanding of the world around them

People of FROB

Mohit Gupta – Founder/Chief Curator

He is that level of a reading enthusiast where his family and friends remove ketchup bottles from the dining table as he may stop eating and start reading the labels on the bottles. A super curious mind Mohit is a on a mission to revive the joy of reading and conversations around books. He is a chemical engineer from Kanpur University and is an alumnus of IIFT, New Delhi where he studied Capital and Financial Markets.

Ajay Kaul – Founding Team Member/Strategy

An icon figure in the world of business, Ajay is one FROB for whom each human being is a book itself. A people’s person he has led many global organizations. He established and expanded the Domino’s Pizza brand in India during his 12 year tenure as the Chief Executive Officer of Jubilant FoodWorks. Under his leadership, Domino’s Pizza became the country’s largest fast-food chain. An IIT Delhi and XLRI alumnus, Ajay’s personal interests include golf, bridge, music, singing and social causes.

Mukesh Kachroo – Founding Team Member/Technology

A globe trotter techie with a very sharp eye for the detail, Mukesh is the backbone of FROB when it comes to IT. A straight talker, Mukesh has led large organizations as a tech leader and is a former CTO of GAIN Capital. He is also the co-founder of Iqwat foundation. Mukesh is an alumnus of BITS Pilani

Ridhima Kaul – Founding Team Member/Operations

The creative hand behind all the FROB’s operations, Ridhima is a design and process enthusiast and can’t stop smiling with joy when she has to manage and curate a face-to-face event. She completed her master’s in business administration from Tarleton State University

Alokik Jain – Content and Media

An entrepreneur from the holy town of Haridwar, Alokik helps FROB with all the content creation. He is a self-taught person and gets deeply immersed into his work but keeps the element of fun intact.
